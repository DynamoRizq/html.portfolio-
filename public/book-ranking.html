The best top 3 books of all time

Riyadh Soliheen by: Yahya Bin Sharaf Al-Nawawi

The title (Riyadh Soliheen) means The Garden of the Righteous, it is one of the most widely
known and read books of hadith all over the world, contaning approximately 1,300 carefully
chosen hadith on ethics, manners.

Think & Grow Rich by: Napolion Hill

According to the author of this book, it was written based on interview of 500 most
afluent men and womanof American during his time. Genrally, Think & Grow Rich it all about;
personal development and self improvement.

Enjoy Your Life by: Dr. Muhammad Al-Arifi

The aim of the book is to trian the reader to enjoy life by practicing various self-development and
interpersonal skills. it is both a practical and systemtic guide to self improvement and a treasure trove of
historical incidents. it increases self-awareness, whilst nurturing the sould and strengthening the spirit.
